Student & Staff Management

Effortlessly manage student and staff profiles, including roles, contact details, and performance records, all in one place.

Stay connected with instant alerts and notifications for updates, reminders, and announcements across the institution.

Simplify fee collection with integrated online and offline payment options, automated receipts, and detailed tracking.

Student Attendance

Track and maintain student attendance records efficiently, with options for manual or automated logging.

Academic Calendar

Manage your institute efficiently with BeeduConnect, designed to simplify and enhance daily operations.

An institute requires extensive effort to manage day-to-day activities and administrative work. Complex paperwork, calculations, analytical reports, and communication between departments are time-consuming processes. An institute management ERP is essential to handle these complexities efficiently.

BeeduConnect stands out with its highly configurable, workflow-based modules, interconnected functionalities, strong communication channels, and data-driven analysis.

BeeduConnect is a distributed system with various roles and responsibilities. It includes multiple portals for Administrators, Office Admins, Principals, Management, Teachers, Accountants, Librarians, Training & Placement Officers, Parents, and Students. Each user accesses their respective portals to utilize the available features.

  • Strong & Dedicated Support Team: Offshore support for quick issue resolution.
  • Ticketing System: Built-in system for issue tracking, supported with dashboards for aging and root cause analysis.
  • Low Maintenance: Configurable system ensures easy maintenance and fast feature availability.
  • Faster Onboarding: Includes data migration templates, predefined onboarding checklists, and onsite implementation support.
  • Strong Governance: Regular status reports, management reviews, and detailed issue resolution plans.
  • Comprehensive Training & Documentation: User training with detailed manuals for reference.

BeeduConnect can be implemented in all types of institutes, from pre-schools to deemed universities. It is enriched with features that support diverse needs and is highly configurable for workflow and functionality adjustments.
