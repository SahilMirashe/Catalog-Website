How it works

3 simple steps to receive payments via Payment Links

Accepting payments is now easier than ever with payment links. Just share a payment link with your customer through WhatsApp, SMS, email, chat, or any medium and get paid instantly!

Step-01

Upload Customer Data

Quickly upload a bulk or batch file with all your customer data in one go. Our system makes it easy to organize and prepare payment requests without manual entry.

Step-02

Approve The Batch

Review and approve the batch with a single click. Our approval process ensures accuracy and gives you control before sending out payment requests.

Step-03

Receive Seamlessly

Once approved, the payment requests are sent to your customers. They can pay instantly, and you’ll receive payments securely and in real time.
