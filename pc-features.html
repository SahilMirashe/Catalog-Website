Simplifying Payment Acceptance

Streamline your payment process with secure, flexible, and convenient solutions for businesses and customers.

The linked based payment collection solution currently is used for Loan repayment by the bank team. With a user-friendly interface, advanced technology and secure integration the solution provides a platform to the bank for uploading data in bulk for generating payment collection link for collection with expiry dates. For additional controls and risk management we have a maker checker feature added.

Role Verification and File Upload

The Maker logs in to verify their role and uploads files for payment link generation in bulk. Files undergo validation, and any exceptions are flagged for review.

Batch and Link Management

Makers can manage batches, monitor link statuses, and track transaction counts post-checker approval. Quick search options are available by Batch ID, Loan Account number, or customer details.

Transactions and Reports

Transaction histories are viewable by date or customer info, and reports can be emailed. Makers can generate detailed reports for reconciliation, settlement, disputes, and refunds, allowing for precise control over transaction management.

Batch Review and Approval

Checkers review and approve uploaded batches, either individually or in bulk. They can download files for inspection, ensuring data accuracy.

Link and Transaction Monitoring

Checkers can monitor approved links, check transaction statuses, and use search filters to locate specific batches or customers.

Reports and Refund Tracking

Checkers access reports on transaction outcomes, settlements, and disputes, with the ability to initiate and track refunds. This supports accurate reconciliation and efficient dispute resolution.

Pay from Anywhere and Pay Device

Customers can make payments from anywhere using their preferred devices, providing flexibility and ease of access for payment transactions.

Partial Payments

Customers can make partial payments towards their dues, offering greater flexibility in managing their finances.

Payment Secured by Payment Gateway

Payments are securely processed through a trusted payment gateway, ensuring the safety and confidentiality of customer transactions.
